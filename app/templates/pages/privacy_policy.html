Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

User Information

  • Account Data: Email address, username, and hashed password
  • Profile Data: Full name, bio, avatar, and preferences
  • Scan Data: URLs scanned, scan results, and security findings
  • Usage Data: Feature usage, login history, and activity logs

Device Information

  • Device ID: Anonymous device identifier
  • IP Address: For security and analytics
  • Browser/OS: Version and type information
We do not collect: Sensitive personal data, financial information, or browsing history beyond what is necessary for the app's functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Process scan requests and generate reports
  • Account Management: Create and manage user accounts
  • Improve App: Analyze usage to enhance features and performance
  • Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Communication: Send updates, notifications, and support messages
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to understand user behavior
Your data is used solely to provide and improve the WebShield Scanner service. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist with:
    • Analytics (anonymous)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
Scan results are private. We do not share your scan results with any third party unless required by law.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS 1.3)
  • Password Protection: Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Authentication: JWT-based authentication with secure tokens
  • Database Security: Secure database with access controls
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and updates
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
We follow industry best practices for data security. Your information is stored securely and accessed only when necessary.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
To exercise your rights: Go to Settings or contact us at support@webshield-scanner.com

6. Data Retention

We retain data for the following periods:

Data Type Retention Period Purpose
Account Data Until account deletion Account management
Scan Results Until account deletion Report access
Audit Logs 90 days Security monitoring
Analytics Data 24 months Product improvement

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Session Cookies: Keep you logged in
  • Preferences: Remember your settings
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect app functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

WebShield Scanner is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for significant changes)

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@webshield-scanner.com
Privacy: privacy@webshield-scanner.com
Website: webshield-scanner.com
