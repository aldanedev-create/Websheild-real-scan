Legal Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024

Legal and ethical guidelines for using WebShield Scanner.

Purpose

This document defines the legal and ethical policies for WebShield Scanner. The app is designed for educational and authorized security testing purposes only.

Legal Warning: Unauthorized scanning is illegal and punishable by law. You are responsible for ensuring you have proper authorization before using this tool.

Ethical Guidelines

1. Authorization Confirmation

Before every scan, users must confirm:

  • They own the website OR
  • They have written permission from the owner

2. Responsible Disclosure

If you discover vulnerabilities:

  • Notify the website owner immediately
  • Allow reasonable time for fixes
  • Do not disclose publicly without permission

3. Professional Conduct

  • Respect website owners and their terms of service
  • Use findings responsibly and ethically
  • Report issues through proper channels
  • Protect sensitive information discovered during scanning

4. Data Protection

  • Protect scan results from unauthorized access
  • Do not share sensitive findings without permission
  • Delete data when no longer needed
  • Comply with data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

5. Educational Purpose

  • Use for learning and education
  • Improve security awareness
  • Develop secure coding skills
  • Share knowledge responsibly

Compliance

1. Legal Compliance

Users must comply with applicable laws:

  • Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Local cybersecurity laws and regulations

2. Jurisdiction

WebShield Scanner is governed by:

  • United States law
  • International cybersecurity standards
  • OWASP guidelines and best practices

3. Acceptable Use Policy

Acceptable uses:

  • Security assessments
  • Education and training
  • Compliance testing
  • Development and QA

Unacceptable uses:

  • Unauthorized attacks
  • Data theft
  • Service disruption
  • Malicious activities

Prohibited Activities

You are strictly prohibited from using WebShield Scanner for:

  • Scanning websites without authorization
  • Attacking, damaging, or disrupting services
  • Performing illegal activities
  • Harvesting data without permission
  • Bypassing security measures
  • Performing denial-of-service attacks
  • Distributing or sharing results without permission
  • Using the app for any malicious purpose
Violation of these prohibitions may result in: Account termination, legal action, and criminal prosecution.
